    About Us

    Welcome to our book exchange community! The founders of our platform, Paul El-Hosni, Nicholas Gochi, Matthew Gutierrez, Daniel Hernandez, Abraham Limon, and Francisco Sanchez, are a driven group of computer science students at CSULA.

    Our website, which is committed to encouraging a love of reading and knowledge sharing, enables users to easily upload their books and associated prices, establishing a book exchange. Discover a wide library which promotes story and idea exchange by allowing all types of books to be easily accessed by a simple search.

    Come together with us as we create a community that values the joy of reading and the ability to interact through the exchange of books.
